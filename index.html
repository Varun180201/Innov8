About Innov8'23

Jecrc University original Technical Fest is finally here. Excited?

Embark on an exhilarating journey of innovation and creativity at "Innov8" – a three-day extravaganza that promises to ignite your imagination and challenge your limits!
Innov8 is not just an event; it's a convergence of eight electrifying events that span the entire spectrum of innovation. From coding clashes that push the boundaries of technology to design duels that shape the aesthetics of tomorrow, each competition is a battlefield of brilliance.
But Innov8 is more than just a series of events – it's an experience. Engage with like-minded innovators, learn from industry experts, and broaden your horizons through workshops and talks that redefine the way you perceive technology.
